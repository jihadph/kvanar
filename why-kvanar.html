Safety at Work

Kvanar Contracting is committed to continuous improvement of safety standards and performance with zero lost time incidents (LTI’s) as our target for every project we deliver. Prevention of accidents is both our moral obligation and practice for good business. Our safety efforts are based on providing every working person a safe and healthy place to work to mitigate project delays and to deliver projects meeting the industry standards and ultimate customer satisfaction.

By providing protection to our employees, property, the public realm and the environment, we ensure that every project we undertake will result in their efficient and successful completion.
